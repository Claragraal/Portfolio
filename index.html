Workflow

  • We discuss your idea (references, tech, deadlines, budget).
  • Sketch / rough preview to lock in style and general idea, heavy changes and revisions are done if needed.
  • Sketch is approved, shading and other details are added and asset is delivered. Small revisions and changes can still be done, but major changes might cost extra.
